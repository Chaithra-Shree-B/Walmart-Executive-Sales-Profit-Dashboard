📊 Walmart Sales & Profit Dashboard (Power BI)

📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Walmart’s sales, profit, and customer data. It transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights to support data-driven decision-making in the retail domain.

🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profitability trends
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Understand customer behavior and segment contribution
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on profit
- Explore regional sales distribution and shipping performance

📈 Key Features
- 📊 Executive overview with key KPIs (Sales, Profit, Margin)
- 🛍️ Product & category performance analysis
- 👥 Customer segmentation and top customer insights
- 🌍 Geographic sales distribution (map visualization)
- 🚚 Shipping mode and delivery performance analysis
- 📉 Trend analysis (monthly/yearly sales & profit)
- 🎛️ Interactive filters (Year, Category, Region, Segment)
  
📊 Dashboard Pages
- Executive Overview – High-level business metrics
- Sales Performance Analysis – Product & category insights
- Profitability – Profit drivers & discount impact
- Customer Analysis – Customer segments & top buyers
- Geo & Shipping – Regional and shipping insights

💡 Key Insights
- Technology and Office Supplies contribute the highest revenue
- High discounts reduce overall profitability
- Consumer segment drives the majority of sales
- Certain regions outperform others in both sales and profit
